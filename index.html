Eric Clapton

About

Eric Clapton is a British rock and blues guitarist and singer. He is the only three-time inductee to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, and has had success both individually and with various acts including The Yarbirds, Cream and Derek and the Dominos. He has recieved many awards throughout his life as a musician, inlcudung 18 Grammys, the Brit Award for Outstanding Contribtion to Music and 4 Ivor Novello Awards. In his solo career, he has sold more than 100 million records world wide.
